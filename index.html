ABOUT US

I am a highly skilled artist, backed by industry qualification and years of experience.

Master nail trainer with a degree from the OZA Art School Russia.

Luminous Nails is in Sunshine Coast providing all nail services including acrylic , Dipping/SNS powder, Hard gel/ Soft gel, polygel, manicure and Shellac/Gel colour.

As a managing director, I have ensured that Luminous Nails has become one of the best nail salons located in the north of Queensland.

There is no rushing at Luminous Nails. I take my time and get the job done properly and safely. I provide the highest quality service with the best long-lasting result.
